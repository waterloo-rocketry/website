INTERCOLLEGIATE ROCKET ENGINEERING COMPETITION

IREC is an international competition hosted by the Experimental Sounding Rocket Association every June in Green River, Utah. The competition is divided into two categories: Basic and Advanced. In both categories, students must submit a rocket purposed to fly up to 10,000' or 25,000' while carrying a 10 lb payload, depending on the category. The competition promotes student design and construction for all of the components, including propulsion, structures, avionics, recovery, and payloads. An over use of commercial components is discouraged, as it does not reflect the spirit of the competition.
